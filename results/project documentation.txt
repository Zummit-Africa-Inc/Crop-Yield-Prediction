
Crop Recommendation 

The main goal of the project is to use technology, mainly Artifical Intelligence in the agricultural sector.

Agriculture plays a vital role in the survival of humanity, and as such, it is extremely important that as an organization with a vision of 'Democratizing AI in Africa,' 
ZummitAfrica is prepared to play it part and take up a resposiblity in using AI in this sector.

This project was created to be able to efficiently and accurately predict the best type of crop to plant in a specific environment given specific climatic and soil conditions.

The datasets used are publicly available datasets.
